Privacy policy

The data collected by this website will be used as a case study in an assessment of the Emotion Ontology.

We will record tags that you apply to presentations, your ratings for the degree of the emotion and your rating of how well you feel the term matched your emotion as well as associated meta data, such as the time that you applied the tag.

All user actions will be anonymized and subsequent publication of the results of this experiment will anonymize conference presentations unless the presenter grants permission for their presentation to be identified.

All data will be stored on a secure server administered by the University of Geneva.

Cookie policy

This website uses session cookies to record login status and to protect against cross-site request forgery (CSRF) only.
